# A10 demo checklist

Record a 60-90 second video showing:

1. App in light mode.
2. Switch OS/emulator to dark mode.
3. Market list, detail page, bet sheet, and portfolio are legible in dark mode.
4. New launcher icon appears on the home screen.
5. App launches with the custom splash screen.
6. Mention or show the release build command output if your instructor wants it in the recording.
